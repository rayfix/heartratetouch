Heart Rate Touch Privacy

Heart Rate Touch does not automatically transmit any user information or health information to Pelfunc, Inc or any 3rd party. Users have complete control of if and how information is shared. Heart Rate Touch does not use analytics to track user behavior or app usage patterns.

In the event of an application crash, Apple, Inc. may collect non-identifiable diagnostics about the nature of the crash for users that have opted-in. Apple, Inc. may provide this diagnostic information to Pelfunc, Inc. in addition to anonymous, non-identifiable information about app sales.
